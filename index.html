Who Moderates My Posts and Timeline?

Moderation on the Fediverse is decentralized: each server (also called an "instance") has its own moderators.

Moderators do the difficult behind-the-scenes work of keeping unwanted content off their server and out of its members timelines. This tool helps you see how the moderation decisions made across the Fediverse come together to influence your personal timeline.

Your local administrators are responsible for maintaining community standards on the server. They can block or suspend accounts, and they can delete posts. They can also block individual users from other servers from appearing on yours or block entire servers.

Moderation is a big job, and the amount of work your administrators and moderators have to do depends largely on the number of active users on your server and how much they post. Moderation reports regarding your posts can also come in from any of the peer servers across the Fediverse.

Decentralized Moderation

Moderation is a difficult task, and many decisions regarding what content meets community standards are inherently subjective. One of the strengths of the Fediverse is the large and diverse set of communities, each with its own standards.

You can help your moderators by reporting unwanted content when you see it. Some servers accept donations, and may even be looking for volunteers to help moderate. Moderation can be a thankless job - try thanking your moderators for the work they do!

How Does This Work?

This tool works by querying the public Mastodon APIs on the account's server; thus, at this time, it is only expected to work with Mastodon instances (sorry). It does so without authenticating, so it can only see information made public by the server admins and/or account holders. Some servers do not publicly report information such as activity, and some accounts have their following and followers relationships hidden.

Privacy

All work is done entirely in your browser; no information is sent to the operator of this website. The only server contacted is the one that hosts the account.
